摘要

High-accuracy image steganography seeks to conceal secret binary messages within a single cover image and recover them with minimal error. However, the pursuit of accuracy often introduces distortions, such as noise and artifacts, increasing the risk of detection. To cope with this issue, we propose an innovative Frequency-guided Iterative network for secure image Steganography, namely FIS, to minimize detectability while preserving visual quality in this paper...
